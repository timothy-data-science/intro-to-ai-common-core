# Lesson 1: Artificial Intelligence in Daily Life

## Duration
2 hours

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
1. Identify AI systems used in everyday applications
2. Differentiate between automation and intelligence
3. Recognise the role of humans in AI-enabled decisions

---

## Lesson Flow

### 1. Icebreaker (15 mins)
Students list mobile apps they use daily and discuss whether AI is involved.

### 2. Concept Introduction (25 mins)
- What is Artificial Intelligence?
- Narrow AI vs General AI
- Common myths about AI

### 3. Case Study (30 mins)
Example: Recommendation systems (e.g. Netflix, Shopee)
- What data is used?
- What decisions are automated?
- Where do humans intervene?

### 4. Group Activity (30 mins)
Students map:
- Input data
- AI component
- Human oversight

### 5. Reflection & Discussion (20 mins)
- One benefit of AI
- One risk of misuse
- Who is accountable?

---

## Assessment (Formative)
- Class participation
- Short written reflection
